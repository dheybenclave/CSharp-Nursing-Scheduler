About
Designed for the smart traveler, Remington Hotel offers superb accommodations at real value.

Remington Hotel is a mid-range hotel that offers real value. It is a suitable option for those looking for affordable hotel accommodations in an excellent location, without compromising comfort and security.

Conveniently located within Resorts World Manila, Remington Hotel is right across Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA) Terminal 3, and a short shuttle ride away from NAIA Terminals 1 and 2. It is also just a few minutes from the Makati central business district via the Skyway. This prime location makes Remington Hotel one of the more popular hotels in Manila among traveling businessmen.

Popular commercial centers, as well as famous landmarks and tourist destinations, such as the Cultural Center of the Philippines and Rizal Park, are all just one cab ride away. Additionally, guests who would prefer to stay closer to the hotel do not need to look far for entertainment, leisure or shopping choices. There are plenty of these available 24/7 just outside the door of Remington Hotel at Resorts World Manila—the Philippines’ first integrated resort.

If you are looking for convenient, comfortable, and affordable hotels in Pasay City, make Remington Hotel your choice. Get in touch with us today to book a room in one of the top hotels near the airport.
